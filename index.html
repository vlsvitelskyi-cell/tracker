06 · FAQ

Questions, answered

How does the Fanvue sync actually work?
You connect through Fanvue's official OAuth — no passwords shared. Fanvue sends a webhook the moment a transaction happens, and the app also polls every 15 minutes as a safety net. If a webhook is ever missed, the next poll catches it.
How are chatters handled?
You assign transactions to a chatter and they earn their personal percentage of that gross — taken off the top before partner splits. The app tracks what they've earned vs what they've been paid, including cash advances a partner fronts before a platform withdrawal (recorded as proper double-entry, so nobody's totals drift).
What do partners and chatters see when they log in?
Partners see their own split, earnings and payout history. Chatters see only their assigned transactions and earnings. Only admins see the whole agency. Data is isolated per agency at the database level — not just hidden in the interface.
Can I track income from outside Fanvue?
Yes — add manual transactions for PayPal, Telegram, wishlists or any custom platform you name. They flow into the same ledger, splits and reports as synced Fanvue income.
Is the exchange rate real?
The USD→EUR/GBP rate is the European Central Bank mid-market reference, fetched server-side and refreshed hourly. If the live rate is ever unavailable the app falls back to an estimate — and labels it as estimated, never as live.
Can I try it before subscribing?
Yes — the full interactive demo runs in your browser with realistic data, no account needed. Every screen, every feature. When you're ready, subscribe through the Fanvue App Store and connect your real earnings.
