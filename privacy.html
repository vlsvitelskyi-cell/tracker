Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 17, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 17, 2026

1. Who we are

Payout Manager ("we", "us", "our") is a financial management tool for Fanvue creators and agencies, accessible at payoutmanager.app. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

For GDPR purposes, the data controller is the individual or entity operating the Payout Manager account (the agency admin). Questions about this policy can be sent to: privacy@payoutmanager.app.

2. Data we collect

Account data. When you register, we collect your email address and the password you set (stored as a hashed value — we never see your plaintext password). This is handled by Supabase Auth.

Agency and financial data. We store the data you enter: agency name, partner names and split percentages, model names, revenue transactions, expenses, and withdrawal records. This data is necessary to provide the service.

Fanvue OAuth data. When you connect a Fanvue account, we receive and store an OAuth access token and refresh token issued by Fanvue. We also store your Fanvue username and creator ID. We never receive or store your Fanvue password.

Usage data. We do not run analytics or tracking scripts. We do not use cookies for advertising. Standard server logs (IP address, browser user-agent, timestamps) may be generated by our infrastructure providers.

3. How we use your data

  • To provide and operate the Payout Manager service
  • To sync your Fanvue earnings via the official Fanvue API using OAuth tokens you authorise
  • To send transactional emails (invite links, account notifications) via Resend
  • To authenticate you and manage your session
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your financial data with third parties except as described in Section 4.

4. Third-party services

We use the following sub-processors to operate the service:

  • Supabase (supabase.com) — database, authentication, and server-side functions. Data is stored on Supabase infrastructure. Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Fanvue (fanvue.com) — OAuth authentication and earnings data API. Subscription billing is handled by the Fanvue App Store. Fanvue Privacy Policy.
  • Resend (resend.com) — transactional email delivery (invite emails, notifications). Resend Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare (cloudflare.com) — DNS and CDN. Cloudflare may process request metadata. Cloudflare Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages (github.com) — static file hosting. GitHub Privacy Policy.

5. Data retention

We retain your account and financial data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we permanently delete all associated personal data — including your email, partner records, transactions, expenses, withdrawals, and Fanvue OAuth tokens — within 30 days.

Some data may be retained longer if required by applicable law (e.g., tax record obligations in your jurisdiction). In that case, we will inform you at the time of account deletion.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — request that we limit processing of your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest

To exercise your right to deletion, go to Settings → About → Legal → Delete my data inside the app, or email privacy@payoutmanager.app. We will process your request within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

7. Security

All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Data at rest is stored in Supabase with encryption. Fanvue OAuth tokens are stored server-side and never exposed to the browser beyond the authenticated user's own session. We apply row-level security (RLS) so each agency's data is isolated and inaccessible to other users.

No system is perfectly secure. If you suspect a security issue, please contact us immediately at privacy@payoutmanager.app.

8. Children

Payout Manager is intended for use by adults operating Fanvue creator agencies. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under the age of 18. If we become aware that a minor has provided personal data, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify registered users by email at least 14 days before the change takes effect. Continued use of the service after that date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

The current version of this policy is always available at payoutmanager.app/privacy.html.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at: privacy@payoutmanager.app.
